Instructions

1. Choose a category from the top from which type of questions you need

2. Select the difficulty you want from newbie, junior or intermediate

3. You can always go back from the difficulty that you have chosen by pressing the back icon.

4. As the quiz starts the timer also will start reducing choose the answer before the timer runs out else the answer will be selected automatically and you will not recieve any points

5. Press the next button to recieve the next question it will appear once the answer has been submitted also when the timer runs out

6. You will recieve a pair of 10 questions from each category. Good Luck!
